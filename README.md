** 📊 HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard
An interactive dashboard built using Power BI and SQL Server to analyze employee attrition and HR trends.

** 🔍 Overview
This project explores employee attrition patterns across departments, job roles, salary, education, and satisfaction levels. Data is stored and queried in SQL Server, cleaned in Excel, and visualized in Power BI.

** 📌 Key Metrics
Total Employees: 10,000  |  Total Attrition: 2,247  |  Attrition Rate: 22.47%  |  Active Employees: 7,753  |  Average Age: 40

** 📊 Dashboard Features
Attrition by Department, Job Role, and Education Level,
Attrition by Overtime, Travel Frequency, and Distance from Home,
Salary Hike % vs Attrition trend,
Years in Current Role analysis,
Job Satisfaction, Work-Life Balance, and Environment ratings.

** 🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI Desktop,
SQL Server,
Microsoft Excel / CSV.

** 💡 Business Problems & Impact
1. Attrition Visibility — The company lacks visibility into how many employees are leaving.
   ➡️ Helps in workforce planning, budgeting, and setting retention KPIs.
   
2. Department-wise Attrition — Which departments are facing the highest attrition?
   ➡️ Enables targeted retention strategies by department.
   
4. Job Role Influence — How does job role influence attrition rates?
   ➡️ Supports redesign of job responsibilities and training initiatives.

5. Distance from Home — Is there a correlation between distance from home and attrition?
   ➡️ Flexible or remote work policies can be targeted to affected employees.
   
6. Overtime Impact — Is overtime contributing to higher attrition?
   ➡️ Promotes balanced workloads and compensation for extra hours.
  
7. Job Satisfaction & Environment — How do satisfaction and environment affect retention?
   ➡️ HR initiatives like culture events and flexibility can be prioritized.
  
8. Work-Life Balance — Are employees with low work-life balance scores more likely to quit?
   ➡️ Wellness programs, work from home, and flexible timing can be introduced.
 
9. Years in Current Role — Does years in the same role correlate with likelihood of leaving?
   ➡️ Career pathing, mentorships, and training can be implemented.
 
10. Salary Hike & Retention — Are salary hikes influencing employee retention effectively?
   ➡️ Aligns compensation strategy with retention outcomes.
